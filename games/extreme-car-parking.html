Extreme Car Parking!

Description

Get ready for the ultimate test of precision driving skills in Extreme Car Parking! This game challenges players to navigate through tight spaces and challenging obstacles to park their cars in designated spots. With realistic physics and stunning graphics, every maneuver requires careful planning and precise execution. Are you up for the challenge?

Controls:

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to control the movement of the car.
Press the spacebar to brake.
Use the "C" key to toggle between camera views for better visibility.
