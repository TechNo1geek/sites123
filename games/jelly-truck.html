Jelly stuck

Description

Embark on a wobbly adventure in Jelly Truck, a whimsical game where you take control of a gelatinous truck traversing challenging terrains! Maneuver your jelly vehicle through obstacles and hazards as you strive to reach the finish line. With its vibrant graphics and quirky physics, Jelly Truck promises hours of fun for players of all ages.

Controls:

Use the arrow keys to accelerate, brake, and steer your Jelly Truck.
Press the spacebar to jump and overcome obstacles.
Balance your truck carefully to prevent it from tipping over.

Tips and tricks:

  • Master the art of balancing to keep your Jelly Truck stable on uneven terrain.
  • Time your jumps wisely to avoid pitfalls and reach inaccessible areas.
  • Collect power-ups scattered throughout the levels to enhance your truck's abilities.

About the creator:

Jelly Truck is developed by JellyCo Games, a team dedicated to creating quirky and entertaining gaming experiences.
