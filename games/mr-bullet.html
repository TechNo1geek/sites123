Mr Bullet

Description

Step into the shoes of the ultimate spy and sharpshooter in Mr Bullet! This action-packed puzzle game challenges players to eliminate enemies with precision and cunning. With its intuitive controls and engaging levels, Mr Bullet offers a thrilling experience for players of all skill levels. Navigate through various obstacles and outsmart your foes to complete each mission and become the top agent.

Controls:

Use your mouse to aim and shoot.
Tap and drag on the screen to adjust your shot trajectory.
Make use of the environment to ricochet bullets and hit multiple targets with one shot.

Tips and tricks:

  • Plan your shots carefully to take out enemies efficiently.
  • Utilize ricochets and environmental objects to your advantage.
  • Take note of the number of bullets you have per level and use them wisely.

About the creator:

Mr Bullet is developed by Lion Studios, a leading developer in mobile gaming.
