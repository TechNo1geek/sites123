Cookie Clicker

Description

Indulge your sweet tooth and embark on a deliciously addictive journey with Cookie Clicker! In this highly addictive idle game, your goal is simple: bake as many cookies as possible by clicking on the giant cookie. As you accumulate cookies, you can spend them on upgrades to increase your production rate, unlock new features, and expand your cookie empire. With each click, watch your cookie count soar and become the ultimate cookie tycoon!

Controls:

Use your mouse to click on the giant cookie to bake cookies.
Navigate the game interface with your mouse to purchase upgrades and manage your cookie empire.

Tips and tricks:

  • Start by clicking rapidly to accumulate cookies quickly.
  • Invest your cookies wisely in upgrades to boost your production rate.
  • Keep an eye on special events and bonuses to maximize your cookie output.

About the creator:

Cookie Clicker is developed by Orteil, an independent game developer known for creating addictive idle games.
