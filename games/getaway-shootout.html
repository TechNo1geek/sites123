Getaway Shootout

Description

Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled showdown in Getaway Shootout! This action-packed game throws you into intense 2D platforming battles against friends or AI opponents. Race through a series of obstacle-filled levels, armed with nothing but your wits and agility. Whether you're competing for glory or survival, Getaway Shootout guarantees non-stop excitement and heart-pounding thrills!

Controls:

Player 1:
- Move: Arrow keys
- Jump: Up arrow key
- Shoot: Right Ctrl
Player 2:
- Move: WASD keys
- Jump: W key
- Shoot: Left Ctrl

Tips and tricks:

  • Master the art of timing your jumps and movements to outmaneuver your opponents.
  • Collect power-ups scattered throughout the levels to gain an advantage over your rivals.
  • Pay attention to your surroundings and use the environment to your advantage.

About the creator:

Getaway Shootout is developed by PixelPlay, a team passionate about creating engaging multiplayer experiences.
