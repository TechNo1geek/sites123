Block the Pig

Description

Block the Pig is a fun and challenging puzzle game that will test your strategic thinking and spatial reasoning skills. In this game, your objective is to prevent a mischievous pig from escaping by strategically placing blocks in its path. With each turn, the pig will attempt to navigate through the maze of blocks to reach the exit. It's up to you to anticipate its movements and block its path to keep it trapped.

Controls:

Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game.
Click or tap on an empty space to place a block.
Click or tap on an existing block to remove it.

Tips and tricks:

  • Plan your moves carefully to create effective barriers that the pig cannot pass.
  • Observe the pig's movement patterns to anticipate its next move and block its path accordingly.
  • Experiment with different block placements to find the most efficient strategy for trapping the pig.
