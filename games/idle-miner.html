Idle Miner

Description

Dive into the world of mining and economic management with Idle Miner! In this addictive idle game, players take on the role of a mining tycoon, overseeing the operation of various mines and expanding their mining empire. Start small with basic resources and gradually upgrade your facilities to unlock new mines, hire skilled workers, and increase production. Watch your profits grow as you automate processes and optimize your mining operations.

Gameplay:

Click on mines to extract resources.
Use earned resources to purchase upgrades and hire workers.
Manage multiple mines simultaneously to maximize efficiency.
Expand your mining empire by unlocking new territories and mining sites.

Tips and strategies:

  • Invest in automation upgrades to increase resource extraction even when you're offline.
  • Strategically allocate resources to balance production and expansion.
  • Complete special objectives and challenges to earn additional rewards.

About the developer:

Idle Miner is developed by IdleCorp, a leading developer of idle and incremental games.
