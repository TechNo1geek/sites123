Basket and Ball

Description

Get ready to shoot some hoops and master your basketball skills in Basket and Ball! This addictive game combines physics-based puzzles with the excitement of basketball. Navigate through a series of challenging levels filled with obstacles, platforms, and tricky pathways. Use your ball's bouncing abilities and gravity to your advantage as you aim for the basket and score points. With its vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Basket and Ball will keep you entertained for hours!

Controls:

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move the ball.
Press the spacebar to jump.
Hold the spacebar to control the power and trajectory of your shot.

Tips and tricks:

  • Time your jumps carefully to avoid obstacles and reach higher platforms.
  • Experiment with different angles and shot strengths to score more baskets.
  • Collect stars scattered throughout each level to unlock bonus content.

About the creator:
