Guess the kitty

Description

Guess the Kitty is a delightful and challenging guessing game that will test your memory and observation skills! In this adorable game, players are presented with a grid of cute kitty faces, each hiding behind a closed door. Your goal is to uncover pairs of identical kitties by revealing two doors at a time. With each correct match, you earn points and uncover more adorable felines. But beware, as the levels progress, the grid becomes larger and the kitties more elusive!

Controls:

Use your mouse or touchscreen to click on two doors to reveal the hidden kitties.

Tips and tricks:

  • Pay close attention to the kitty faces behind each door to remember their locations.
  • Start with smaller grids and gradually increase the difficulty as you improve your memory skills.
  • Take your time and avoid rushing through levels, as accuracy is key to earning high scores.
