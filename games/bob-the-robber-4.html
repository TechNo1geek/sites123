Bob The Robber 4

Description

Join Bob the Robber in his daring adventures in Bob The Robber 4! As a master thief with a heart of gold, Bob embarks on a series of heists to outsmart security systems and steal valuable treasures. Navigate through various levels filled with guards, security cameras, and traps, using your wits and stealth skills to avoid detection and reach your objectives. With each successful robbery, unlock new challenges and advance Bob's thrilling story!

Controls:

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move Bob.
Press the spacebar or "Up" arrow key to interact with objects and doors.
Use the "Z" key or "Ctrl" key to use tools and gadgets.
Press the "R" key to restart a level if needed.

Tips and tricks:

  • Study the level layout and patrol patterns of guards to plan your moves carefully.
  • Use hiding spots and distractions to evade detection and sneak past security.
  • Collect coins and valuable items to unlock upgrades and enhance Bob's abilities.

About the creator:

Bob The Robber 4 is developed by Kizi Games, a leading developer of casual and adventure games.
