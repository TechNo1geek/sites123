Crossy Road

Description

Embark on a charming and addictive journey with Crossy Road! This delightful game combines simple yet engaging gameplay with colorful voxel graphics. Your goal is to guide a variety of characters across a busy highway, avoiding traffic, crossing rivers, and navigating through challenging terrain. With each successful crossing, you'll earn points and unlock new characters to play.

Controls:

Tap or click anywhere on the screen to move your character forward.
Swipe left, right, up, or down to change direction.
Time your movements carefully to avoid obstacles and hazards.

Tips and tricks:

  • Patience and timing are key; wait for safe openings to cross roads or rivers.
  • Collect coins along the way to unlock new characters and environments.
  • Watch out for speeding vehicles, trains, and other obstacles that can quickly end your run.

About the creator:

Crossy Road is developed by Hipster Whale, an indie game studio known for its innovative and quirky titles.
