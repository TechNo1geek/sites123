Getting over it

Description

Prepare yourself for a uniquely challenging experience with Getting Over It. In this unconventional game, players control a man stuck in a cauldron, armed only with a large hammer. Your objective? Scale a seemingly insurmountable mountain of junk using only your hammer and your wits. With intentionally frustrating physics and minimalistic graphics, Getting Over It tests your patience, determination, and ability to overcome frustration like few other games.

Controls:

Use the mouse to control the movement of the hammer.
Move the mouse cursor to swing the hammer and propel yourself upwards.
Master the art of precise movements to navigate the obstacles and progress.

Tips and tricks:

  • Embrace failure as part of the learning process. Every fall teaches valuable lessons.
  • Take your time and plan each move carefully. Rushing can lead to costly mistakes.
  • Stay calm and focused, even when faced with seemingly impossible challenges.

About the creator:

Getting Over It is the brainchild of game designer Bennett Foddy, known for creating uniquely challenging and thought-provoking experiences.
