Burnin Rubber 5 XS

Description

Prepare for adrenaline-pumping action in Burnin Rubber 5 XS! This high-octane racing game puts you behind the wheel of powerful vehicles as you compete in thrilling races and intense combat challenges. With stunning graphics and immersive gameplay, Burnin Rubber 5 XS delivers non-stop excitement for racing enthusiasts.

Controls:

Use the arrow keys to steer your vehicle.
Press the "X" key to accelerate.
Use the "Z" key to brake or reverse.
Press the spacebar to activate turbo boost.
Use the "C" key to shoot weapons and engage in combat.
Press the "V" key to switch weapons.

Tips and tricks:

  • Master drifting around corners to maintain speed and control.
  • Collect power-ups scattered throughout the tracks to gain advantages over your opponents.
  • Upgrade your vehicle with better weapons and performance enhancements to dominate the competition.

About the creator:

Burnin Rubber 5 XS is developed by Xform, a leading developer of action-packed racing games.
